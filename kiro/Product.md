## General City Behavior
- Bengaluru traffic is non-linear: small triggers cause large jams
- Rain instantly doubles travel time after 5:30 PM
- Locals optimize for predictability, not shortest distance
- Crowd presence = safety signal (food + routes)

## Street Food Intelligence

### Time-Based Safety
- Chaat & pani-based food safest: 6:00–9:00 PM
- After 9:30 PM, avoid water-based items (pani puri, chutneys)
- Fried items (bajji, pakoda) safer during rain

### Stall Reputation Signals
- ≥10 locals standing = high trust
- High turnover > visual cleanliness
- Avoid stalls with pre-filled pani containers
- Gas stove preferred over coal after sunset

### Area Rules
- Indiranagar 100ft Rd: good until 9:30 PM
- MG Road: tourist-heavy, inconsistent hygiene
- Whitefield: avoid late-night street food entirely
